Limitations and Concerns


The following chapter provides notes on general limitations (especially those that were identified at the time of Study Planning) and detailed concerns that occurred during analysis and may present potential origins of bias. All notes have been assigned to a level of estimated potential impact (Major, Medium, Minor).

General / A-priori Limitations

Limitation Comment

Detailed Concerns

Concern Potential Bias Type Remedial Measure
Major
Disease onsets may be assumed wrongfully (as too late). Therefore, calculated onset intervals may be incorrect.

If a patient has more than one distinct cancer entity, therapy measures can not be linked to different entities.

Medium
No causal attribution of complication occurrence to Chemotherapy

Hormone therapy is not assessable (no specific OPS codes)

Minor
Age at Admission not exact due to lack of exact birth date.

Occurrence of implausible HIV coding.

Postal code of patient taken from first admission, although moving between admissions is imaginable.
